First Ever in Myanmar

QuadCycle Innovations, founded by 6 passionate young people who care deeply about sustainability, is the first ever in Myanmar to operate Recycling Vending Machines. Recycling Vending Machines work just like normal vending machines with an additional feature of being able to instantly reward to those who bring back the purchased products.
